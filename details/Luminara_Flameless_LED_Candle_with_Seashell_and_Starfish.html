Luminara Flameless LED Candles

Elevate the cozy vibe of your living space with these beautifully crafted flameless LED candles. Designed to resemble the serene beauty of the ocean, they come in a soft sea blue hue and are adorned with hand-selected miniature seashells and starfish, adding a charming coastal feel to any room. Unlike traditional candles, these are free from open flames and make an ideal choice for safe home lighting, especially in areas where children or pets are present.

Key Features

  • Real wax construction for a natural, lifelike appearance
  • Moving flame technology for realistic illumination
  • Soft chambray design with embedded seashell and starfish
  • Unscented and safe for homes with kids or pets
  • Long-lasting 200 hours with automatic shutdown

Why Choose Luminara?

Whether you're looking to bring a touch of seashore charm to your bedroom or decorate a bookshelf with a safe and elegant glow, these candles are the perfect addition. Their 6.5-inch height and 3.5-inch diameter offer a balanced, eye-catching design that works well as a centerpiece. With an included timer function, you can set them to shine for 5 hours and then automatically rest for 19, maximizing their usability over time. These flameless candles make a thoughtful gift for any special occasion, from birthdays and Easter to Mother's Day and housewarmings, offering both style and safety in every light.
